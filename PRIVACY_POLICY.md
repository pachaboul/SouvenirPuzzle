# Privacy Policy — Souvenir Puzzle

_Last updated: 2026-07-08_

**Published online:** https://pachaboul.github.io/SouvenirPuzzle/privacy/

Souvenir Puzzle ("the app") is published by **Koyrasoft**. This policy
explains how the app handles your data. In short: **everything stays on your
device. We do not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.**

## Data we collect
**None.** Souvenir Puzzle has no servers, no user accounts, no analytics, no
advertising, and no tracking. The app works fully offline.

## Photos
- You choose photos from your device to turn them into puzzles.
- A local optimized copy and a thumbnail are stored **only in the app's private
  storage on your device**.
- Your original photos in the gallery are never modified or deleted.
- Photos are **never uploaded** anywhere.

## On-device data
Game data (created puzzles, results, best times, settings, optional profiles) is
stored locally in the app's private database. You can delete a single memory, or
erase everything from **Settings → Delete all memories**. Uninstalling the app
removes all of this data.

## Permissions
The app requests access to your photos/gallery for the sole purpose of letting
you pick an image to play with.

## Children
The app is suitable for all ages and does not collect any data from anyone,
including children.

## Contact
Questions about this policy: **koyrasoft@gmail.com**

## Changes
If this policy changes, the updated version will be published at the same URL
with a new "Last updated" date.
